Reading notes & caveats

  • Pooled mvp-032 Lift mixes metrics. The tool averages boolq (accuracy) and gsm8k (exact_match) pairs into one number (−0.069). Read per-task: boolq +0.064 mean, gsm8k −0.334 (n=1). The cards above are per-task.
  • Unbalanced valid seeds. gsm8k with-skill valid on {s1,s2}, without-skill valid on {s0,s2} — only s2 pairs. boolq is the only fully-paired task (both seeds, both cells).
  • Two samsum trajectories were backfilled post-hoc (with-s0, without-s1) — they never ran finalize, so their analyzer artifacts were regenerated from the surviving journal; status shows crashed.
  • gsm8k-with-skill-s1 is the 17h run recovered after an OOM+restart (status crashed, wall 2874s reflects the truncated restart, not the graded 17h work).
